Astrix, is an Israeli electronic trance music DJ and producer psytrance specializing in the subgenre of Progressive psychedelic trance. In 2006 Astrix reached in DJMag's "Top 100 DJs" yearly rank along with a rise of several other Israeli musicians (the highest being Infected Mushroom ranked #9)[1] marking a significant proportion of the Israeli electronic music scene.
